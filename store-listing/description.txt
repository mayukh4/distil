Distil replaces your new tab page with a clean, distraction-free productivity dashboard. Every time you open a new tab, you see the time, a personalized greeting, live weather, and a curated feed of arXiv research papers, Google News headlines, or Hacker News top stories — all rendered in a sleek glassmorphism interface with monospace typography over a dark space background.

Built for researchers, developers, and anyone who wants their browser to work harder for them.


WHAT YOU GET

Time, Date & Personalized Greeting
A large, elegant clock with a contextual greeting that changes based on time of day — "Good Morning," "Good Afternoon," or "Good Evening" — personalized with your name.

Live Weather
Current temperature, conditions, and a minimalist weather icon. Powered by OpenWeatherMap with support for Celsius and Fahrenheit. Updates automatically every 30 minutes. Enter any city worldwide.

Pomodoro Focus Timer
A built-in 25-minute focus / 5-minute break Pomodoro timer with an animated circular progress ring. Start, pause, and reset directly from the new tab page. Notifications alert you when a session ends. The timer persists across tab opens and browser restarts — it keeps running even when you close the tab.

Persistent Notes
A quick-access notepad that auto-saves as you type. Expand or collapse it with one click. Export your notes as a .txt file at any time. Perfect for capturing ideas, links, code snippets, and to-dos without leaving your browser.

arXiv Research Papers Feed
Browse the latest papers from arXiv.org across 44 categories spanning Physics, Computer Science, Mathematics, Quantitative Biology, Statistics, Economics, and Electrical Engineering. Select only the categories you care about — from Machine Learning and AI to Cosmology and Algebraic Geometry. Papers display titles, authors, dates, and category tags with direct links to arXiv.

Google News Headlines
Stay current with headlines from Google News across 8 topics: Technology, Science, Business, Health, World, Nation, Entertainment, and Sports. Select your preferred topics in settings. Each article shows the source outlet, publication date, and topic tag.

Hacker News Top Stories
The top stories from Hacker News with point scores, comment counts, author names, and direct links to both the article and the HN discussion thread.

Quick Links
Add up to 8 custom shortcut bookmarks displayed as favicon circles below the clock. Drag to reorder. One click opens your most-used sites — GitHub, Gmail, Notion, Slack, or anywhere else.

NASA Astronomy Picture of the Day
Toggle a stunning daily space photograph from NASA's APOD service as your new tab background. Includes an info overlay with the image title, explanation, and date. Uses a resilient multi-tier fetch pipeline with automatic fallbacks.

Zen Mode
Press the Z key to enter Zen mode. Everything fades away except a large centered clock and date — no panels, no widgets, no distractions. Press Z or Escape to return. Your new tab becomes a calm, focused space.

Settings Panel
Click the Distil icon in your toolbar to configure everything: your name, quick links, arXiv categories, news topics, weather city and units, API key, and APOD background. Every change takes effect instantly without reloading.


DESIGN

Glassmorphism design system — frosted glass panels with translucent backgrounds and subtle borders layered over a dark backdrop. The default background is a photograph of the M87 black hole, replaceable with NASA's daily astronomy image.

JetBrains Mono typography throughout. Vibrant orange accent color for active states, tags, and highlights. Fully dark-themed interface with smooth animations.


PRIVACY

Your data stays local. Notes, settings, and cached feeds are stored on your machine using Chrome's built-in storage. Distil does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. No accounts. No ads. No tracking. No analytics.


WEATHER SETUP

Weather requires a free OpenWeatherMap API key:
1. Sign up at openweathermap.org (free tier)
2. Copy your API key
3. Paste it into Distil settings under Weather
4. Enter your city name
